upcycled & handmade goods

Handmade in Cheshire, England

Saved&made use discarded and surplus materials to create rugged practical items and equipment for every adventure.

The materials are mostly sourced from used tents, truck tarpaulins, and deconstructed army surplus. I try to preserve original features where possible, preserving authenticity and adding to the identity of each item. In some products, you might see the outline of an old fastening, the evidence of something removed, or even a secret pocket!
